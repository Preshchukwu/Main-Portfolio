About Me

I am a self-taught Product Designer. My journey began in October 2020 when i came across a design challenge online. Intrigued by the prospect of creating intuitive and visually appealing digital experiences, I decided to take a shot at it.

With no prior experience or formal training, I threw myself into the world of design. I took it upon myself to gain knowledge through various online courses and boot camps on UI/UX principles, design thinking, and user-centered design. I practiced tirelessly, designing and redesigning interfaces and gathering feedback from peers and online communities.

As I delved deeper into the field, I discovered my passion for creating digital products that solve real-world problems and delight users. I honed my skills in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design, I also have basic knowledge of HTML and CSS, constantly pushing the boundaries of my abilities.

I am now ready to take the next step in my career and I am ready to apply for entry-level UI/UX product design roles. I am excited to contribute my skills and creativity to a team and gain valuable experience in the industry. I am confident in my abilities and also eager to continue learning and growing as a designer.
