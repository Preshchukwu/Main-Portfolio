About Pick Cash App

Pick Cash is a loan application that is designed and developed to help users to sign in using their BVN . By using the Pick Cash application, user's spendings and transactions are recorded to build credit card score/points to accord users with the opportunity to choose from a loan class according to their spendings. With Pick Cash, lending money is easy and navigating the app is seamless.

Background

As our final project in our Product Design class, my team members and i were assigned to design a loan / credit app in 7 days. An app that signs the user up with their BVN and record their spendings, build credit scores / points and offers them a credit class they can take loan from. The main aim was to make money lending easy and intuitive so we needed to create an app that has a seamless navigation.

Problem Statement

Due to the length of time it takes to access loans and the extensive personal information needed, users are dissatisfied with the loan app they are currently utilizing. Also individuals are unaware of their access limits because they are unable to control their expenditures.

Objectives and Goals

  • The primary goal was to make money lending easy and intuitive by creating a seamless navigation
  • To create an app that allows users sign up with their BVN, record their spending. build scores and points, offer credit class for users to take loans

Roles

This was a collaborative project with contributions from different designers. We were grouped in teams with different tasks assigned to each to enable us work productively. This project taught us the importance of team effort.

From evaluating the application funnel to conducting user interviews to uncover any potential areas of friction preventing users for completing their loan requests and ultimately proposing solutions to address the issues we uncovered.
